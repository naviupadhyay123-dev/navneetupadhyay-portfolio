About me

Hey there! I’m Navneet Upadhyay, an artist, designer, and creative explorer from Jaipur, India — someone who believes that art isn’t just made with colors, but with emotions, patience, and imagination. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications (BCA) from Dr. K. N. Modi University, where I built a strong foundation in technology — but my heart always leaned towards visual creativity. Over time, I merged both worlds: art and tech, to create designs that not only look beautiful but also tell a story.
